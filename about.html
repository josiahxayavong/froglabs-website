About us

A small studio with a high standard.

Frog Labs LLC develops modern software with a focus on high-quality mobile applications, productivity tools, and innovative digital experiences. We believe great software is quiet: it loads instantly, respects your privacy, and gets out of your way.

Our story

Frog Labs started with a simple frustration: too much software is built to be shipped, not to be used. Features pile up, interfaces grow noisy, and the person holding the phone gets forgotten.

We took the opposite path. Every Frog Labs product begins with a single question — what would make this feel effortless? — and everything else follows from the answer. That means smaller apps that do their job brilliantly, interfaces you can learn in seconds, and updates that make things better without making them heavier.

Our first flagship product, Blackjack for iPhone, reflects that philosophy: a complete, polished game with a built-in strategy trainer and detailed statistics, all in a clean vertical interface that works entirely offline.

Our principles

What guides everything we build

Craftsmanship

We sweat the last 10 percent — the animations, the edge cases, the empty states — because that's where quality lives.

Simplicity

Features earn their place. If something doesn't make the experience clearer or faster, it doesn't ship.

Privacy

Your data belongs to you. Our apps are designed to run on your device, not on our servers.

Longevity

We build software meant to be kept for years, and we support it that way — with steady updates and responsive help.

Respect for users

No dark patterns, no attention traps, no surprise charges. We treat users the way we'd want to be treated.

Constant curiosity

We prototype, test, and explore relentlessly. The lab in Frog Labs isn't just a name.

See the philosophy in action

Blackjack for iPhone is the clearest expression of how we build: focused, fast, and finished.
