FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Does Blackjack involve real money?

No. Blackjack is for entertainment and practice only. All chips are virtual, there is no way to wager or win real money, and no payment is ever required to keep playing.

Where are my statistics stored?

All statistics and progress are stored locally on your device. Nothing is uploaded to our servers. This also means that deleting the app permanently removes your statistics, so keep that in mind before reinstalling.

Can I play without an internet connection?

Yes — the entire app works offline, including gameplay, statistics, and the strategy trainer. No connection is ever required.

How do I reset my statistics or bankroll?

Open the app's settings and choose the reset option. Resetting is permanent and cannot be undone, so the app will ask you to confirm first.

Will my progress transfer to a new iPhone?

If you restore your new iPhone from a device backup (iCloud or computer), your app data comes along with it. Installing the app fresh from the App Store starts with a clean slate, since data is stored only on the device.

How do I suggest a feature?

We genuinely want to hear your ideas — many of our favorite features started as user suggestions. Email contact@froglabsllc.com with the subject "Feature request."

Troubleshooting

Common issues and quick fixes

Most problems are solved by one of the steps below. If yours isn't, email us — include your iPhone model and iOS version and we'll dig in.

The app won't open or closes immediately

Try these steps in order:

  1. Close the app fully (swipe it away in the App Switcher) and reopen it.
  2. Restart your iPhone.
  3. Check the App Store for an app update, and Settings → General → Software Update for an iOS update.
  4. If it still fails, email us — please don't delete the app first, as that would erase your local statistics.
Animations feel slow or choppy

Low Power Mode reduces animation performance system-wide. Check Settings → Battery and turn off Low Power Mode, then relaunch the app. Closing other heavy apps running in the background can also help on older devices.

My statistics look wrong or stopped updating

Fully close and reopen the app first — statistics are written continuously but summaries refresh when a session ends. If numbers still look wrong, take a screenshot of the statistics screen and email it to us so we can investigate.

My progress disappeared

Progress is stored on your device, so it can be lost if the app was deleted and reinstalled, or if you set up your iPhone as new instead of restoring a backup. If neither happened and your progress is still missing, contact us with your iOS version and roughly when you last saw your data.

Something else is wrong

Email contact@froglabsllc.com with a description of the issue, your iPhone model, your iOS version, and a screenshot if possible. The more detail you include, the faster we can fix it.
