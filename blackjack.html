FAQ

Frequently asked questions

Is this real-money gambling?

No. Blackjack uses virtual chips only, for entertainment and practice. There is no real-money wagering, no way to deposit money, and nothing to win or lose except your practice bankroll.

Do I need an internet connection?

No. The entire game — gameplay, statistics, and the strategy trainer — works completely offline. Your data stays on your device.

What is the basic strategy trainer?

Basic strategy is the mathematically optimal way to play every blackjack hand. The trainer deals you scenarios, asks for your decision, and instantly tells you whether it matched the correct play — so perfect strategy becomes second nature.

What statistics does the app track?

Hands played, wins, losses, pushes, blackjacks, win rate, best streaks, and your strategy trainer accuracy over time. All statistics are stored locally on your device.

Which devices are supported?

Blackjack is designed for iPhone and supports recent versions of iOS. Check the App Store listing for the current compatibility details.
